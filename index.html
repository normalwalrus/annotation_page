How to transcribe
  • Listen to the whole clip before saving. Replay as often as you need.
  • Type exactly what you hear, in normal sentence case with punctuation.
  • Numbers: write them the way they were spoken — “twenty five” if said as words, “2 5” if spelled out.
  • Unclear word? Give your best guess in brackets: [unclear] or [market?].
  • Nothing intelligible at all? Use Can't make it out — that answer counts too.
  • Shortcuts: Ctrl+Space play/pause · Ctrl+← restart · Ctrl+Enter save.
